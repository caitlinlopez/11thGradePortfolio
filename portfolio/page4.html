summer research connection with caltech astrophysics

This past summer, I had the opportunity of having an internship with Caltech Astrophysics. I worked with a team consisting of a professor, three grad students, and another high-schooler intern to design and program a website. We implemented HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to display information about Dr. Gregg Hallinan's team's research on the radio sky. The link to the website can be found below:

http://www.tauceti.caltech.edu/ovro-lwa-sky/index.html

To find out more about Dr. Hallinan's team's research, visit http://www.tauceti.caltech.edu/LWA/
